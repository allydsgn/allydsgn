My name is Allyson Arrogante, and I'm a designer, illustrator, visual communicator, and organization lover. I can draw, lay things out neatly, and make information easier to understand.

My illustrations vary in style and can be done digitally or traditionally depending on the style needed for the project. My designs are always organized, have purpose, and continually get "wow"s from my peers. I lay out information to make it easier to digest and make it more appealing to those that need to remember it.

For a little insight of how to deal with me... I'm a five foot filipina-american that loves art, video games, music, learning, and a neat workspace. If I'm not available I'm either sleeping, on a design adventure, or venturing a digital one. If you really want to woo me then please provide a very meaty burger to my address with extra well-done french fries. If this meal also contains Red Robin seasoning and campfire sauce then consider us best friends.
